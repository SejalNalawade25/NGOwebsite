About Us

  • About

    • Pasaydan Seva Prakalp is a non-governmental organization that works towards improving the lives of orphans and underprivileged children in India. Despite its noble mission, the NGO is currently facing several challenges that are hindering its ability to effectively serve the community. One of the main issues facing Pasaydan Seva Prakalp is a lack of financial resources. The organization relies on donations from individuals and organizations to fund its various programs and initiatives. However, with the COVID-19 pandemic and the resulting economic downturn, donations have decreased significantly, leaving the NGO struggling to meet its financial obligations.

In addition to financial challenges, Pasaydan Seva Prakalp also faces logistical issues due to its location. The NGO is located in a remote area that is far from the city, making it difficult to access. This can make it challenging to bring in supplies and resources, as well as to transport children to and from the facility. Another issue facing Pasaydan Seva Prakalp is a lack of proper infrastructure. The organization's facilities are currently housed in temporary structures with aluminium roofing sheets, which do not provide adequate protection from the elements. This can make it difficult for the children to learn and play in a comfortable and safe environment.
Despite these challenges, Pasaydan Seva Prakalp remains dedicated to its mission of helping orphans and underprivileged children. The NGO is committed to providing these children with the basic necessities of life, including food, clothing, and education, as well as healthcare and vocational training. We, therefore, appeal to the general public to donate generously towards this noble cause. Your contribution, no matter how small, will help Pasaydan Seva Prakalp to continue its work and make a positive difference in the lives of these children. Together, we can create a brighter future for the next generation.

Pasaydan Seva Prakalp Poineer

Mr.Govardhan Darade

"Myself Govardhan Darade I reside on a hilltop in the small village of Khadki in Beed District. Coming from a poor family, my parents used to work as Sugarcane cutting workers. Despite all this, I managed to pass my 10th and at age of 16 I started working with a sickle. I continued cutting off Sugarcane for 2 long years, which was a family tradition. Like struggling for 2 long years, I shifted from my Sugarcane cutting worker to vehicle cleaner and then to the Mechanics side in charge of a company. Struggling for years and learning many things, I still used to feel conscious. I always used to think that the kind of environment I grew up I didn't want anyone to face that kind of situation anymore. And then I thought of taking the task of shaping their life into my hands. Around that time I left my well-paying job and started NGO Pasaydan Seva Prakalp. Our NGO has already shaped various life and will do the same in the future. And this would be successful by kind helping hands" -Mr.Govardhan Darade
